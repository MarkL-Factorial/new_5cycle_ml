# Project Guidelines

## Language Rules

- **Thinking, documentation, code, and scripts**: English only.
- **Discussion with the user**: Traditional Chinese (繁體中文) or English is acceptable.
- **Technical terms / proper nouns**: Always use English (e.g., `DataFrame`, `classification`, `pipeline`), even within Traditional Chinese sentences.
- **Strictly prohibited**: Simplified Chinese, Japanese, Korean, or any other language not listed above.
